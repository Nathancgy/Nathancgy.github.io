Welcome to OpenTeens

OpenTeens aims to encourage teenagers to understand more about open-source technology and build a thriving community to collaborate on exciting projects. Realizing the importance of open-source technology in the real world of coding and the lack of accessible open-source resources for teenagers, I took the initiative to establish a youth-led open-source community. OpenTeens now has its official website, an active social media account, a communication platform, and an APP prototype. Ultimately, I want to make a lasting impact on the open-source landscape for teenagers.
